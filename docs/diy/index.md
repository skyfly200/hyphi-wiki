# DIY Build Guides

<StatusBadge type="wip" />

Step-by-step guides for building Hyphi hardware yourself. Whether you ordered bare PCBs or are sourcing your own components, these guides walk through everything from tools to final test.

## Before You Start

These guides assume basic electronics comfort — if you've soldered before, you're set. If not, read [Soldering Tips](./soldering) first.

::: tip Bare PCBs
Hyphi sells bare (unpopulated) PCBs for makers and artists who want to build their own. Check [hyphi.art](https://hyphi.art) for availability.
:::

## What You'll Need

At minimum:
- Soldering iron with fine tip (Hakko FX-888D or similar)
- Solder — 63/37 or 60/40, 0.5mm diameter
- Flux pen
- Tweezers (ESD-safe)
- Multimeter
- USB-C cable (data-capable)

For SMT / 0402 work:
- Hot air station
- Solder paste (Sn63Pb37, refrigerated)
- Stencil (order from JLCPCB with your PCB)
